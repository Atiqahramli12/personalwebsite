4. 'SUKOL' Competition 2019

Picture 9: Tun Ali College team

Picture 10: Handball team

Picture 11: Group photo

Picture 12: 'SUKOL' parade night performance

Picture 13: Our team's medal

SUKOL is an 'inter-college sports' competition organized by UiTM Melaka student sports committee. The competition held various matches such as handball, netball, futsal, kayak, volleyball and the best performance of the night of the inter-college parade. Since at that time we did not have students in college, we decided to send our own members for each game. Ever since school, I've been wanting to go play handball and I'm not going to miss the chance to get into this competition. Our college has sent all of us for handball, netball, kayak and futsal matches. We're so grateful we didn't expect to have won for each of those games. We've got runners-up for handball and netball matches, 3rd place for kayak matches and even the best parade performances. It was a very meaningful experience for me because while we were tired of learning, we also remained supportive of each other. Click here to watch our 'SUKOL' preview.

5. Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Event

Picture 14: Group photo

Picture 15: Picture with students and guests

Picture 16: Distribute foods for breaking fast

Picture 17: During the event

Picture 18: The Quran hour

Our CSR event has been named as 'Dapur Kasih' programme held during the month of Ramadan 2019 where we will distribute food for breaking fast to all UiTM Alor Gajah residents for free. In addition, this program aims to give zakat contributions to the students and staff of UiTM itself. After breaking fast, we conducted a 'Quran Hour' programme to revive the Quran during the month of Ramadan. We managed to run this csr program with only 24 members of tun ali college. We also collaborates this programme with MY Hope Hero and M.O.V.E.
